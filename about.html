Welcome to Vardhaman Gold

At Vardhaman Gold, we believe that jewellery is more than just an accessory—it's a celebration of life's most precious moments. For over decades, we have been crafting exquisite pieces that blend traditional artistry with contemporary elegance, bringing you the finest collection of 22KT pure gold and bridal jewellery.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create jewellery that captures the essence of Indian heritage while embracing modern aesthetics. Today, we stand proud as one of Pune's most trusted names in gold jewellery, serving generations of families who have made us a part of their special occasions.

Our Heritage

Rooted in the rich cultural tapestry of India, every piece at Vardhaman Gold tells a story. Our master craftsmen, with their decades of experience, meticulously handcraft each design with precision and passion. From intricate Bengali bridal sets featuring delicate filigree work to contemporary diamond pieces that shimmer with modern sophistication, our collection embodies timeless elegance.

We take immense pride in preserving traditional jewelry-making techniques—from the authentic Ahmedabadi die-work to the mesmerizing Kolkata sheet work—while continuously innovating to meet the evolving tastes of our discerning clientele.

Our Promise

Quality and purity are the cornerstones of everything we create. Each piece of jewellery at Vardhaman Gold undergoes rigorous quality checks and comes with a hallmark certification, ensuring you receive nothing but the finest. Our commitment extends beyond the product to encompass a complete experience—from personalized consultations to after-sales service that builds lasting relationships.

Our Collections

Bridal Jewellery

Our bridal collection is a celebration of love and tradition. From elaborate multi-layered haars adorned with intricate floral motifs to elegant chokers featuring authentic die-work, each piece is designed to make your special day unforgettable. We offer complete bridal trousseau sets including necklaces, earrings, bangles, maang tikkas, and more—all crafted to perfection.

Traditional Gold Jewellery

Experience the timeless beauty of traditional Indian gold jewellery. Our collection features authentic Bengali Shakha bangles, elegant Pola bangles with sheet work, magnificent raani haars with drop detailing, and stunning kadas with peacock and elephant motifs. Each piece honors the rich heritage of Indian craftsmanship.

Contemporary & Diamond Jewellery

For those who prefer modern elegance, our contemporary collection offers sleek designs that blend seamlessly with today's fashion. Our diamond jewellery pieces in rose gold and white gold feature exquisite craftsmanship with floral patterns and geometric designs, perfect for both traditional ceremonies and modern celebrations.

Visit our showroom in Pune to experience our exquisite collection firsthand, or connect with us to learn more about our latest designs.
