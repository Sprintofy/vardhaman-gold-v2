About Us

At Vardhaman Golds, purity isn’t just a promise — it’s our foundation. We are a Pune-based jewellery house dedicated to accurate computerized gold purity testing and authentic hallmark-certified jewellery at the most reasonable making charges.

Every ornament we craft reflects honesty, skill, and tradition, ensuring our customers experience not just beauty, but complete transparency and trust.

“We don’t measure gold by its weight — we measure it by your trust.”

Our Vision

What We Offer

At Vardhaman Golds, we combine technology, tradition, and trust to deliver the finest experience in gold purity and hallmark jewellery.

1. Computerized Gold Purity Testing
  • We use advanced computerized machines to check the exact purity of your gold — no guesswork, no hidden details.
  • Our testing process is transparent, precise, and trustworthy, ensuring you always know the true value of your gold.
  • Bring gold from anywhere — we’ll test it with 100% accuracy and honesty.
2. Hallmark-Certified Jewellery
  • Each piece of jewellery crafted at Vardhaman Golds is BIS hallmark-certified, guaranteeing purity, authenticity, and long-lasting value.
  • From elegant rings to royal necklaces, we design jewellery that reflects class, culture, and confidence.
  • Pure gold. Certified quality. Crafted to perfection.
3. Minimal Making Charges
  • We believe beauty shouldn’t come at a heavy price.
  • That’s why we create every ornament at the lowest making charges in Pune, without compromising on design or purity.
  • Premium designs, honest pricing — because trust is priceless.
4. 100% Satisfaction Guarantee
  • Customer satisfaction is at the heart of everything we do.
  • If you’re not satisfied, we offer a full money-back guarantee — no questions asked.
  • Because your trust matters more than profit. Your confidence is our real hallmark.
5. Old Gold Testing & Exchange
  • Have old gold jewellery? We’ll test its purity and offer fair value if you wish to sell or exchange it.
  • Every transaction is done openly and ethically, so you always get what your gold is truly worth.
  • Fair valuation. Transparent exchange. Complete peace of mind.
6. Custom Design Creations
  • We also craft custom-made hallmark jewellery designed exclusively for you.
  • Our artisans combine modern style and traditional artistry to make ornaments that suit every occasion.
  • Your idea. Our craftsmanship. One timeless creation.
